Lesson 2 Exercises - Challenges

We'll use JavaScript to convert temperatures from Celsius (°C) to Fahrenheit (°F). The formula is:
  • Fahrenheit = (Celsius * 9/5) + 32
  • Celsius = (Fahrenheit - 32) * 5/9

  1. The temperature is 25°C. Calculate the temperature in Fahrenheit(77).
  2. The temperature is 86°F. Calculate the temperature in Celsius(30).
  3. The temperature is -5°C. Calculate the temperature in Fahrenheit(23).
